Virtual and Augmented Reality (VR/AR)- Know more

VR and AR are transforming the shopping experience by allowing customers to virtually try on clothes or accessories. Virtual fitting rooms and AR-powered apps help shoppers visualize how an outfit looks on them without physically trying it on, improving convenience and reducing returns.
